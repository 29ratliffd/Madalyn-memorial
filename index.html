Madalyn Marie Korte- in memoriam

Humble beginnings

On november 6th, 2009, Madalyn Marie Korte was born, at her birth she was 8 pounds- your average baby. At the time her parents Jennifer Korte and Keaton Korte had 2 children, Lance Korte, who is currently a band major at ohio state university, and Nolan Korte, who is currently enrolled at milton-union highschool. While Madalyn would grow up to have a 3rd brother, he wouldnt be born for another 2 years. Madalyn was born with nice blonde hair and blue eyes, as seen in figure 1. Madalyn would then be taken back to her parents home on rangeline road, where she would spend her years growing until she got to the ripe age of 5, where she was enrolled for school.
